Our Services

Your Local Experts in Structural Steel Fabrication and Installation

At Techno-Weld Limited, we are your local structural steel fabrication company in Mississauga, Ontario offering supply, fabrication, installation and welding of structural and miscellaneous steel. We go beyond just supplying the industrial steel and aims to customize and modify it according to your industrial requirements. Being in service for more than a decade, local builders and contractors constantly rely on us to provide the material and service needed to get the job done on time and in the budget.

Structural Steel & Miscellaneous Steel Fabrication

Working from solid plans, we convert raw steel into cut, drilled, welded, modified and painted frames ready to deliver. We specialize in cutting and modification of steel stair, steel railing, hand railing, metal deck, steel gates, steel fences, RTU frame and all types of welding, except stainless steel and aluminum.

Our design builds and fabrication team well understands what it takes to facilitate a steel erection process. At Techno-Weld Limited, our fabrication services include our ability to provide miscellaneous steel products to not only fulfill your needs but to reflect special project needs.

Structural steel Design & Fabrication supply goes for:

Commercial warehouse

We fabricate and supply durable structural steel components tailored for large-scale commercial warehouses to ensure long-lasting strength and stability.

Mezzanine Floor

Our custom steel mezzanine floors maximize vertical space while maintaining superior load-bearing capacity and precision engineering.

Canopy

We design and fabricate sturdy steel canopies that provide both protection and architectural appeal for commercial and industrial structures.

Steel Joist

Our high-quality steel joists are manufactured to meet exact design specifications, ensuring structural integrity and optimal load distribution.

Metal Deck

We supply and install steel metal decking systems that enhance structural performance and serve as a solid base for concrete slabs.

Joist Reinforcement

Our team provides reliable joist reinforcement solutions to strengthen existing structures and meet upgraded load requirements.

Gratings

We design and fabricate sturdy steel canopies that provide both protection and architectural appeal for commercial and industrial structures.

Handrails

We craft precision-engineered steel handrails that meet safety codes while complementing the aesthetic of your structure.

RTU Frames

We fabricate RTU frames designed for rooftop units, ensuring strong support and stability for HVAC and mechanical systems.

Molds

Our steel molds are engineered with precision for consistent and accurate shaping in various construction and manufacturing projects.

Ladders

We build steel ladders that combine safety, functionality, and durability for industrial and commercial environments.

Bollards

Our fabricated steel bollards provide protection, traffic control, and a clean, professional finish to property perimeters.

Gates & Grills

We manufacture robust and customizable steel gates and grills that enhance both security and design.

Railings

Our steel railings are expertly crafted to provide safety, strength, and a refined appearance for stairways, decks, and platforms.

Finishing, Welding & Installing Steel Products

After the products are fabricated, welded and finished, we install them as per your requirements. Most often, our standard finish is a prime paint that is applied to enhance the finish, which in turn improves the beauty and protection such as galvanized or powder coat. Some of the products we weld and install include:

  • Stairs – concrete fitted pan stairs, bar grate
  • Ladders – caged, elevator pit, roof hatch
  • Railings – ramp rails, picket, mesh, z-line pipe
  • Bollards – embedded, surface mounted, decorative
  • Loading Dock Frames
  • Tools – custom, replacements
  • Machinery – bracing, mounts, parts
  • & Many Others
